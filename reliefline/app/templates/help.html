Help

ReliefLine supports the relief supplies pipeline for Urdaneta City, Santa Barbara, and Calasiao, under the coordination of the Provincial Social Welfare and Development Office (PSWDO).

Signing in

Use the username/email and password issued to you by your office administrator. If you've forgotten your password, use Forgot Password on the login page.

What each office can do

  • PSWDO — monitors province-wide warehouse stock, approves or rejects relief requests, and coordinates pre-positioning and distribution.
  • CSWDO/MSWDO — submits relief requests for its own municipality, reviews barangay damage reports, and monitors its own municipal food pack stock.
  • Barangay-level users — confirm that food packs reached their intended recipients.

Need more help?

See Contact Support to reach the right office for your question.
