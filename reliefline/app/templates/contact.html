Contact Support

For account access, password issues, or technical support with ReliefLine, reach out through your own office's usual chain:

  • Barangay-level users — contact your City/Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO/MSWDO).
  • CSWDO/MSWDO personnel — contact the Provincial Social Welfare and Development Office (PSWDO).
  • PSWDO personnel — contact your ReliefLine System Administrator.

Locked out of your account? Use Forgot Password on the login page to reset it yourself.
