Privacy Notice

ReliefLine is a restricted-access system used by the Provincial Social Welfare and Development Office (PSWDO), City/Municipal Social Welfare and Development Offices (CSWDO/MSWDO), and authorized barangay personnel of the Province of Pangasinan to coordinate typhoon-related relief operations.

What we collect

  • Account information for authorized users — name, email, and office or barangay assignment.
  • Disaster response records submitted through the system — barangay damage reports, relief requests, allocation and distribution records, and warehouse stock levels.
  • Basic system activity logs (who approved or updated a record, and when) kept for accountability.

How it's used

Data in ReliefLine is used solely to plan, coordinate, and monitor food pack allocation, pre-positioning, and distribution during typhoon-related disaster response in the Province of Pangasinan. It is not shared outside the PSWDO, CSWDO/MSWDO, and barangay offices involved in relief operations.

Access

Access is limited to authorized personnel with an assigned account. Every account's actions are logged for accountability, consistent with the system's role-based design.
