Terms of Use

By accessing ReliefLine, you agree to use the system only for the authorized disaster-response coordination duties assigned to your role — PSWDO, CSWDO/MSWDO, or barangay-level personnel — in the Province of Pangasinan.

Account use

  • Accounts are personal and must not be shared.
  • Reports submitted through the system — damage assessments, relief requests, and distribution/validation records — must reflect actual, good-faith information.
  • Unauthorized access or use of ReliefLine outside of official relief operations is prohibited.

System availability

ReliefLine requires an active internet connection. It is provided as a decision-support tool — its predictions and recommendations are estimates and do not replace the professional judgment of PSWDO, CSWDO/MSWDO, and barangay personnel.
